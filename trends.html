LATEST TRENDS

OUTFITS

TOPS

  • This summer tube tops are very popular, whether those being with simple designs or a little bit more unique ones.
    Embroided tops like the third picture are very in style right now, especially ones that are hand-made.
    Black&White is also coming back this summer as they are so easy to pair with any bottom.
    Lastly the vintage tops like the fifth picture are coming back in style, with their interesting designs and breathable materials.

BOTTOMS&DRESSES

Tight long floral dresses seem to be very trending in the fashion world for this summer, as they are absolutely everywhere in every clothing store. I don't know if they are a micro-trend for this summer and the next they will be out of style, but as for now they are maybe the number one latest trend in fashion.

Long flowy light colored skirts are in style again this summer as they are absolutely practical and the material that they are usually made from is light, breathable and reduces sweating. But also they are really easy to pair with any shirt, therefore they won't be out of style anytime soon.

SHOES&BAGS

Chunky bags and kitten heels are my favourite fashion trend right now. There are so colorful and bright that they will make the simplest of outfits chic.
